Introduction

Hello everyone,
KCET (populerly known as K-CET) is one of the most trusted examinations conducted to get into a under graduation course,here we try to give an insight of what we are upto.

About CET

As mentioned Earlier, K-CET is a good platform to get into UG(under graduation courses) in colleges across the state,here we have retricted ourselves to B-Tech as this is just a demo.

Reservation

As of now, there are no Special privilages given to any category or a religion ,But that could be an updated version of the current version.

How to Apply

Applying for K-CET is not so difficult , If you have not yet created your account ,Then create a new account by "clicking on the link on the right corner", And give the neccesary deatails and you are all set.

Eligibility

Those who have completed second year PUC or any other eqivalent exams with the authorized board in the post two years are the eligible to apply.However, here we shall restricted ourselves to KARNATAKA STATE(PUC), CBSE and ICSE boards.

Criteria

The criteria for seat allotment is solely based on the workes obtained in the K-CET exam. As mentioned earlier ,Here we are not providing any kind of special previlages to any persons.so,do well in your test and " ALL THE BEST! "
