Karnataka Common Entrance Test

INSTURCTIONS

  1. This quetion paper consits of three sectiond namely.
    • Mathematics
    • Physics
    • Chemistry
  2. Each section will have 20 quetions each.
  3. You will be awarded with 1 mark for every correct answer and there is no negative marking.
  4. All the quetions will have 4 options, out of which only one of them is correct.
  5. You are supposed to select the option, which you feel correct.
  6. The duration of the test is only 60 minutes.
  7. If you have finished the paper within the 60 minutes, you can always use the END TESTbutton to end the test.
  8. After 60 minutes the test will be terminated it's own!.




Instructions for option entry

  1. The candidates have to enter their choices in the decreasing order of their priority.
  2. That is, they should enter their first choice as the most preferred choice and so on.
  3. A candidate can enter any number of preferences he/she wants to
  4. The allocation of seat is dependent on the number of seats available in the college for that branch
  5. The allocation process starts by giving the highest scored candidate the first preference and so on
  6. However, once the options are submitted you cannot change your preferences as there is only one round of allocation
  7. So make sure that you give your order of preferences appropriately

Important note:

a seat will only be allocated if you have taken the test. In case you have enterred your choices of preferences but not taken the test, then no seat shall be allocated even if there is availablility of seats

However, there is no rule that you should take your test first and then enter your choices. You can first enter your choices and then take the test as well
